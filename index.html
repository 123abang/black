    Black Santa FAQ

    What is Black Santa?
    Black Santa is the cutest and most engaging meme coin on the blockchain. It's a community-driven project dedicated to creating a fun, engaging ecosystem while supporting charitable causes worldwide.
    When was Black Santa founded?
    Black Santa was founded in September 2024, originally branded as FluffCoin. As of September 16, 2024, we are now live with our new brand, Black Santa, evolving from our roots to bring even more joy, community engagement, and value. Our mission remains focused on building a strong, reputable brand and fostering a vibrant community in the crypto world.
    What makes Black Santa unique?
    Black Santa combines the light-hearted nature of memes with the serious potential of blockchain technology. We focus on community engagement, charitable initiatives, and financial opportunities for our holders.
    How does Black Santa support charitable causes?
    7% of our total supply is allocated to supporting community-focused causes globally. We partner with various organizations to provide funding for impactful initiatives, making a positive difference in the world.
    What is the total supply of Black Santa?
    The total supply of Black Santa is 1,000,000,000 BLACK SANTA tokens.
    How are the Black Santa tokens distributed?
    Community Rewards: 10% (100,000,000 BLACK SANTA)
    Development and Marketing: 10% (100,000,000 BLACK SANTA)
    Charity and Donations: 5% (50,000,000 BLACK SANTA)
    Liquidity Pool: 15% (150,000,000 BLACK SANTA)
    Team Allocation: 10% (100,000,000 BLACK SANTA)
    Ecosystem Growth: 45% (450,000,000 BLACK SANTA)
    What security measures are in place for Black Santa?
    Black Santa has achieved KYC certification through Assure DeFi, ensuring trust and credibility within our community. Additionally, our liquidity is locked, guaranteeing the stability and security of our token. We have also ensured that there are no more mintable tokens, providing peace of mind to holders by preventing any unexpected inflation of the supply.
    What are the future plans for Black Santa?
    Our roadmap includes:
    - Expanded listings on more decentralized exchanges.
    - Launch of staking rewards once the community grows.
    - Implementation of multi-language support for global outreach.
    - Increased support for impactful community initiatives.
